How WEARC online exam sessions work

Our sessions are remote, friendly, and structured so you know exactly what to do. The most important step is to use the official session page for payment and registration.

Two-step sign-up

Registration checklist

  • Choose a session date on HamStudy.
  • Pay the exam fee using the link(s) on that page.
  • Complete registration right after payment.

You should receive a confirmation email from ExamTools within a few minutes. If it is missing, check spam, then email us.

What to expect

During the session

We will verify your identity, walk through the process, and help you feel comfortable before the exam begins.

  • Plan to join a little early so your audio and video are working.
  • Have a government-issued photo ID available.
  • Pick a quiet space where you can focus.
  • Follow the exact instructions in the session notes on HamStudy and ExamTools.
Multiple exam elements: If you want to take more than one element in the same session, email us ahead of time so we can schedule enough time.
Study

Use HamStudy to prepare

Practice until you are consistently passing. Then schedule your exam with confidence.

Tip: Focus on understanding, not memorization. It will make operating easier later.

Ask

We help first-timers

If something is unclear, email us. We would rather answer questions early than fix last-minute issues.
