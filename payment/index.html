Payment Requirement

Payment is required within 1 hour of registration. If payment is not completed, we may remove the registration to make space for other candidates. Exams may be rescheduled within the same calendar year if requested at least 3 hours in advance; all payments are non-refundable. If you have any questions or encounter technical issues with payment, please reach out to the team and we will gladly provide assistance.
