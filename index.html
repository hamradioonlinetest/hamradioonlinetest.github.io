Fast sign-up

What to do next

  1. Pick a date on the HamStudy WEARC sessions page.
  2. Pay the exam fee using the payment link(s) on that session page.
  3. Complete your registration right after payment.

You should receive a confirmation email from ExamTools within a few minutes. If you do not see it, check your spam folder.

FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to live in New Jersey?

No. Our remote sessions are open nationwide. Choose a session and follow the registration steps on the HamStudy page.

What are the sign-up steps?

There are two key steps: pay the exam fee using the link(s) on the session page, then complete your registration right after payment.

Can I take more than one exam element?

Yes, sometimes. Email us ahead of time so we can plan enough time for you during the session.

I registered but did not get a confirmation email. What now?

Confirmation emails typically arrive within a few minutes. Check your spam folder first. If it is still missing, email us and we will help.

How do I reach the team?

Email hamradiotest@wearc.org. You can also call or text Mike at +1-917-502-2203.
