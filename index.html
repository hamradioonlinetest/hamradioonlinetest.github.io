FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to live in New Jersey?

No. Our remote sessions are open nationwide. Choose a session and follow the registration steps on the HamStudy page.

What are the sign-up steps?

There are two key steps: pay the exam fee using the link(s) on the session page, then complete your registration right after payment.

Can I take more than one exam element?

Yes. Email us ahead of time so we can plan enough time for you during the session.

I registered but did not get a confirmation email. What now?

Confirmation emails typically arrive within a few minutes. Check your spam folder first. If it is still missing, email us and we will help.

How do I reach the team?

Email hamradiotest@wearc.org. You can also call or text Mike at +1-917-502-2203.

Where can I find a ham radio exam session or VE session near me?

Visit the WEARC HamStudy listing to see the current ham radio VE session schedule, including remote and online options. Sessions are open nationwide.

What do I need to bring to a ham radio licensing exam (ID requirements)?

Bring a legal photo ID, your FRN if you already have one, and any Certificates of Successful Completion of Examination (CSCEs) from prior elements.

Is an FRN required for ham radio exam registration?

Yes. You need an FCC FRN to register for an amateur radio license exam. If you do not have one yet, register for an FRN online before the session.

What is the ham radio exam fee?

The exam fee is $15 for any exam element. If you pass, you can take the next higher element(s) in the same session at no additional cost through Amateur Extra.

How do I complete ham radio exam registration?

Select a date, pay the exam fee, and complete the registration form right after payment. This secures your spot on the exam session schedule.

Do you offer technician, general, and amateur extra class license exams online?

Yes. Our volunteer examiner sessions can cover Technician, General, and Amateur Extra exams. Email us if you plan to take multiple elements.
