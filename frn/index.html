Enter the 10-digit FRN used for your exam. The FCC application results will open in a new tab.

After the FCC results open:
  1. Open the newest amateur-radio application associated with your recent exam.
  2. Select the Admin tab and review the history section.
  3. For a new license, when event status = Redlight Review Completed, that is the cue to submit payment.
  4. When event status = Paperless Authorization Printed, that is typically the final step and your call sign or new privileges should be visible in the FCC ULS.
New license

New Technician license applicants

After the VE team completes the exam session, the results move through the VEC and then to the FCC. The FCC application may not appear immediately.

When the FCC makes the application fee available, log in to FCC CORES using the same FRN and complete the $35 FCC application payment.

The exam-session fee and the FCC application fee are separate payments. Do not send the FCC payment to WEARC or the ARRL VEC.

After the FCC confirms payment and grants the application, your new call sign will appear in the FCC license records.

Upgrades

General and Amateur Extra upgrades

A normal license-class upgrade for someone who already has an amateur radio call sign does not normally require the new-license FCC application fee.

Use the FCC Application Search to follow the modification application, and use the FCC license records to confirm that the license class has been updated.

If the FCC itself presents a charge in CORES, follow the FCC instructions shown there.

FCC events

Common FCC application events

New Application Received
The FCC has received the application.
Redlight Review Completed
The FCC has completed an initial financial-review step. A new-license applicant should check FCC CORES for a pending application fee.
Payment Confirmed
The FCC has recorded the application payment.
Application Granted
The new license or license-class upgrade has been granted.
Paperless Authorization Printed
The FCC generated the electronic authorization record.

The wording and timing shown by the FCC may vary. The FCC website is the authoritative source for the application and license status.

Help

Troubleshooting

No FCC application appears

The VEC may not have submitted the session yet, or the FCC may still be processing the submission. Check again later. Weekends and federal holidays can affect processing.

The application appears, but payment is unavailable

The application may still be moving through the FCC’s initial review. Check FCC CORES again later.

Payment was completed, but no call sign appears

Payment confirmation and license grant are separate steps. Check the FCC application history and license records again later.

Existing license upgrade

Search for the modification application and then confirm the updated license class in the FCC license record.

FAQ

FCC application questions

Where do I search by FRN?

Enter your 10-digit FRN in the form on this page. The official FCC application results will open in a new tab.

Where do I pay a new-license FCC fee?

When the FCC makes the application fee available, log in to FCC CORES with the same FRN and follow the FCC payment instructions.

Where will my FCC application results appear?

The application results appear on the official FCC website in a new browser tab. Open the newest application associated with your recent exam, then select Administrative History to review its progress.
