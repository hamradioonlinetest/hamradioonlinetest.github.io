Before you join

Quick prep overview

You will need two devices with cameras, most commonly a laptop and a smartphone. The laptop is your primary Zoom connection, while the smartphone provides a second camera view. Have a small stand ready so the phone can be positioned quickly when asked.

Install Zoom on both devices and confirm you are on the latest version. Right before the session, close any non-critical applications so Zoom has full access to camera and audio. Keep your desk clear and make sure your exam room is private for the entire session.

Timing reminder: Complete your registration and payment at least one hour before your scheduled exam time.
Checklist

Online exam session requirements

  • Must have two devices with cameras (typically a laptop and smartphone).
  • Small stand for the smartphone for easy positioning.
  • Zoom installed on both devices, updated to the latest version.
  • All non-critical applications closed or exited before joining the session.
  • Desk cleared of all papers and non-essential items.
  • Room is private: only the candidate is in the room during the exam.
  • Registration and payment completed at least 1 hour prior to exam time.
  • Email the VE team ahead of time if you plan to take more than one exam element.
  • Only request additional elements if you are ready (a good target is scoring above 85% on practice exams).
